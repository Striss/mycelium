Prime Facts

  • A prime number is a natural number greater than 1 that has no positive divisors other than 1 and itself.
  • The first few prime numbers are 2, 3, 5, 7, 11, 13, 17, 19, 23, and so on.
  • Primes are the building blocks of all natural numbers as every integer greater than 1 is either a prime or a product of primes (Fundamental Theorem of Arithmetic).
